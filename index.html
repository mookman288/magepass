Getting Started

First time using MagePass? MagePass can manage your passwords locally or remotely. For more information and an in-depth guide, visit the official documentation.

Local Storage

If you want to store your passwords locally using the default configuration, simply click Create New Account in the menu.

Please note that if you store your passwords locally, you won't be able to access them cross-platform unless you setup a remote solution.

Remote Storage

Remote storage can be accomplished one of two ways, with the official remote storage service MageTomb, or you can sync your local store with a third-party application.

If you've setup MageTomb, or you've purchased a service that provides MageTomb, you can configure your application settings to use MageTomb instead of local storage. Click Settings and configure your endpoint before creating or logging into your account.

Do you have your own solution for remote storage, such as automatic syncing software? You can store your passwords locally but select a destination to sync with. Click Settings in the menu to configure your storage location. Please note that spinning up your own solution is not recommended.
